Drug Discovery, Development, and Deployment Map biologics map. 
The map comprises neighborhoods, each of which consists of a complex network of steps that interact with steps in other neighborhoods. 
The primary distinguishing steps between biologics and small molecules are in neighborhoods C (lead identification) and D (lead optimization).
